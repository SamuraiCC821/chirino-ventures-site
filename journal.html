Still warm.

This page holds the notes, experiments, and updates that still have some heat on them. Not polished. Not final. Just honest records of what is happening while it is happening.

On building in public.

There is something useful about putting the work out before it is ready. Not because rough work is better — it usually isn't — but because the act of showing it creates a kind of accountability that private notebooks don't. You stop pretending things are more finished than they are.

This site is that for me right now. The structure is visible. The gaps are visible. I am filling them in real time, and anyone who finds this page can watch that happen.

What I keep coming back to.

The way Corpus Christi looks in the hour before a storm. The smell of salt and diesel near the ship channel. What it means to come from a family that built things with no safety net and kept going anyway. The difference between infrastructure that holds and infrastructure that merely appears to hold.

These are not themes I chose. They are just what I find myself thinking about when I stop performing and start writing.
