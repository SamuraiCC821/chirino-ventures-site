Statement of Purpose

Chirino Ventures LLC

Purpose

Chirino Ventures LLC exists to manage, safeguard, and cultivate private family capital. The Company maintains a long-term perspective rooted in discipline, simplicity, and resilience.

Guiding Principles

— Long-term value creation
— Privacy and discretion
— Sustainable and patient decision-making
— Stewardship over short-term gains
— Ethical and disciplined operations

Mandate

Chirino Ventures LLC is a privately held, veteran-owned family office. We do not solicit outside investors or offer services to the public. All capital allocations and private initiatives are conducted internally at the sole discretion of the Company.
