Experiments — Things I am trying

Rough edges
showing.

A lot of my process is making order out of something rough. These experiments are where that happens in public — before the edges are fully cleaned up, before I know whether any of it works.

This site.

The first experiment is this one. A static site with no framework, no CMS, no analytics, no external calls. Every font is self-hosted. Every style is hand-written. I wanted to understand what I was actually building, not delegate that to a tool and hope for the best.

What I learned: the constraints force decisions. When you cannot reach for a library, you understand why the library exists. When you cannot install a plugin, you understand what the plugin was hiding from you.

What comes next.

More work that starts in uncertainty and figures itself out in public. Infrastructure ideas. Community tools. Writing structures that might be useful to other people who are building without a map.

I will put them here as they become worth showing.

Work in progress — always.
