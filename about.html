About | Christian Chirino

The person
behind the site.

Born in Miami, not long before Hurricane Andrew. My dad came from Cuba during Mariel. My mom from Colombia. He pumped gas. She cleaned offices on weekends. There was no inherited path, just people who kept going, and the understanding that you figure it out or you don't.

The Navy came next. After that, eight years running IT infrastructure for forty-plus clients across South Texas. Both taught me the same thing: understand what you are working with before something breaks, and stay calm when it does anyway.

How I think.

I default toward clarity. Confusion has a real cost, in time, in trust, in the quality of whatever you are trying to build. I would rather say a hard thing plainly than wrap it in language that softens the edges until no one knows what was actually said.

I believe in structure, enough of it that the work can hold up on its own, not so much that it becomes the point. And I believe in motion: keep going even when the plan is incomplete, because waiting for certainty is usually just a way of not starting.

Why this site.

I built this to have somewhere honest to put things I was actually thinking about. Not a portfolio in the traditional sense. More like a record, of what I am paying attention to, what I am trying, what I believe to be true right now.
