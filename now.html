Now | June 2026

What has my
attention.

Corpus Christi. June. The kind of heat that tells you exactly where you are. I am still running forty-plus clients, still showing up every day. But the work I am more focused on right now is longer range. Who knows me. Who trusts me. What that is worth.

In progress.

Building reputation deliberately. Not chasing visibility for its own sake, but being intentional about who I am around, who I am learning from, and what kind of work I want to be known for. Networking on the Gulf Coast is different from networking anywhere else. It is slower, more personal, and it sticks when it is real.

This site is part of that. A place that says what I actually think, in the voice I actually have. That matters more than a polished resume.

Paying attention to.

Water. Corpus Christi sits on a bay and the water issues here, access, quality, coastal infrastructure, are real and not talked about enough. I am watching what is happening locally and following the broader conversation around Gulf Coast resource management.

Team USA in the World Cup. Soccer built differently when the stakes are that high and the country is finally paying attention. It is worth watching closely.
